## 4. Team Norms and Operating Guidelines

### Meeting Cadence
* **Frequency:** Twice a week
* **Duration/Format:** Depending on the assignment

### Communication Guidelines
* **Primary tools:** Discord
* **Response time expectations:** Within 24 hours
* **File sharing/documentation:** Discord, additional services if needed

### Behavioral Norms/Expectations
* **Attendance:** Group members will attend all team meetings and let teammates know if they will miss a meeting.
* **Participation:** Group members will collaborate and discuss assignments together to ensure we are all on same page and communicate with each other when working parts of project.
* **Accountability/quality standards:** Work will adhere to assignment requirements.

## 5. Decision-Making Process
* **How decisions will be made:** Discussion and consensus.
* **Escalation process:** Evaluate opinions and decide which is the best one in a way that everyone agrees.

## 6. Conflict Resolution Plan
1. Evaluate ideas and opinions
2. Listen openly and respectfully
3. Think about what is best for the project/assignment
4. **Escalate to team lead or sponsor if unresolved:** Matt / Poul

## 7. Key Resources / Support

* **Access/Credentials:** Github, Discord
* **External Support:** Poul, NSC Tutors
